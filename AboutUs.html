About Melbourne Public Library

 
Melbourne Public Library was established in 2016 to help readers start reading books and continue reading from our following Categories: Fiction, Science Fiction, Science, Management, History, Art and Technology. Library services team also provide bulk loan books to local residents and deliver books free of charge to senior member's homes and collect when finished reading as part of community services.
Melbourne Public Library plans to bring latest technology call EBook for all our readers that they can read books online as part of our service. by using there portable device, Computers, Tablets & smart Phones.
Talk to Us it is easy and free to all local residents. Reading gives our brain a different kind of workout than watching TV or listening to the radio.

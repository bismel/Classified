Welcome to Melbourne Public Library

We're glad you are here! Education does not have to cost thousand of dollars; learning can be done for free. The concept of letting anyone, regardless of their financial situation, gain knowledge is the basis behind social institutions such as Melbourne Public Library.
Our public library is a credit to the communities that we serve and anyone who likes to read and learn should be a member. Parents who homeschool should be especially adamant about getting their children used to library from the time that the children are young. Making the library a part of childhood makes it's more likely that people will continue to use the library even once they are adults.
Melbourne Public Library gives local resident the option to rent and read books for free by becoming a member, library is credit to community certainly take advantage of our offerings.
Melbourne Public Library opens it's door to education and opportunity and this two things are invaluable for enriching one's life.
